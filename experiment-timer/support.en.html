Contact

For questions, bug reports, or feature requests, please reach out:

  • Email: support@cedarlab.dev

We typically reply within 1–3 business days.

Note: To report inappropriate ads specifically, you can also use the dedicated channel inside the app at Settings → Report inappropriate ad (per Apple Guideline 2.5.18).

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Where are my timer settings and history stored?

Timer settings, categories, sessions, and history are stored only on your device and are not uploaded to any server by the App. (Note that in the free tier, advertisements served by Google AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as IDFA), usage data, diagnostics, and approximate location data on the AdMob side; see the Privacy Policy for details.)

Q. Can I migrate my data to a new device?

The current version stores data locally only and does not offer automatic device-to-device migration. We recommend exporting important records via the Pro CSV export feature if you need to keep them.

Q. I purchased Pro but I still see ads

If your purchase has not been applied immediately, please tap "Restore Purchase" on the Settings screen. If that does not resolve the issue, please contact us via the support email above.

Q. What does Pro include?

  • CSV export of experiment records (not available in the free tier)
  • Unlimited saved user-created timers / protocols (free tier is limited to 5 timers and 2 chained sessions)
  • Extended history retention (free tier: 1 day / 7 days → Pro: 30 days / unlimited)
  • Ad-free experience
  • Lock Screen display of remaining timer time (Live Activities; supported devices only)

One-time purchase, no recurring charges (your local App Store price is shown on the in-app purchase screen). Family Sharing is disabled.

Q. Can I use the sample timers (TBST wash / Centrifugation, etc.) in the free tier?

Yes. The 5 pre-installed sample timers do not count against the "5 user-created timer" free-tier limit and can be used without restriction. You can edit or delete them freely.

Note that the 1 sample session (Secondary antibody + wash) counts toward the regular "2-session" free-tier limit, just like any other active session. With the sample session present, you can create up to 1 additional session before reaching the cap.

Q. Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple under their refund policy and may be available depending on the circumstances. Please request a refund directly via Apple: https://reportaproblem.apple.com

Note that the developer cannot process refunds.

Q. What happens to my data after a refund?

Your timer settings and history data are not deleted after a refund. Pro features (CSV export, unlimited saves, long-term history, ad-free, lock-screen display) are turned off, but any timers you created remain usable. When you create or duplicate new timers, the free-tier limit (5 user-created timers) applies again. Repurchasing Pro restores full access.

Q. How do I delete all of my data?

Uninstalling the App will delete all local data associated with the App.

Q. Do timer completion notifications work in the free tier?

Yes. Timer completion alarms and Lock Screen notifications work in the free tier. Pro adds Lock Screen display of the remaining time during a running timer (Live Activities) on supported devices.

Q. I don't get notifications when a timer ends

Please check that notifications are allowed for Experiment Timer under iOS Settings → Notifications → Experiment Timer. Lock Screen, Notification Center, and Banner display all need to be enabled. Note that Do Not Disturb and Focus modes can also suppress notifications.

Q. Does the timer keep accurate time while the app is in the background or the screen is locked?

By design on iOS, the in-app counter pauses while the app is in the background or the screen is locked, but a local notification is scheduled for the timer's end time so the alarm still fires at the configured time. The elapsed time is recomputed when you reopen the app.

Q. A timer result looks unexpected / the app crashed

Please report it via the support email with the reproduction steps (which timer, the configured values, and what happened). Including the App version and iOS version is helpful too.
