ResearchCalc

A calculator built for researchers in life sciences. iPhone — iOS 17+.

ResearchCalc handles the calculations that come up every day in the lab: dilutions, molar concentrations, statistics, and more. Results stay on the same screen and can be reused in the next prep with one tap. Works fully offline.

Built-in Features (Free)

  • 13 unit conversions: dilution (C₁V₁ = C₂V₂), molar / mass stock prep, %w/v / %v/v, serial dilution, antibody ratio dilution, ×N stock dilution, mass ↔ moles, specific gravity, A260 / A280 / OD600 readouts.
  • 39 built-in compound presets (NaCl, Tris, HEPES, DMSO, etc.) with molecular weight and density included.
  • Basic and scientific calculator modes with statistics (mean / SD / SEM / CV%).
  • Local history (recent 7 days, up to 200 entries) and on-screen result memos (up to 3).
  • Light, Dark, and (iOS 18+) Tinted appearance.

Pro (¥600, one-time purchase, no subscription)

  • 7 more conversions: reagent batch prep, cell seeding, cell density (hemocytometer), concentrated acid / base dilution, % additive (original / final basis), and in-vivo dose conversion (mg/kg).
  • Add and edit your own compound presets.
  • Keep history without a time limit (up to 200 entries), with CSV export.
  • Pin up to 15 result memos.
  • Customize the keypad layout and advanced statistics (median / IQR / 95% CI / geomean, etc.).
  • No advertisements.
