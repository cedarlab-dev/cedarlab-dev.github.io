LabCountimer

A multi-timer and counter app for lab experiments. iPhone — iOS 15.1+.

LabCountimer helps researchers manage the overlapping wait times in PCR, Western blot, ELISA, cell culture, and other lab workflows. Every step can be a timer, a counter, or both — so one chained session captures “wash 3×, 5 min each,” counting and timing together. Works fully offline; no account required.

Features (Free)

  • Run multiple timers and counters at the same time.
  • Combine a timer and a counter in a single step (e.g. “wash 3×, 5 min each”).
  • Chain timers and counters into reusable chained sessions (multi-step timer/counter workflows) and save them to your library.
  • Completion notifications that fire even in the background.
  • Experiment history retained for 1 or 7 days.
  • Light and Dark appearance.

Pro (one-time purchase, no subscription)

  • CSV export of experiment records.
  • Unlimited saved custom timers and chained sessions (free tier: up to 5 timers and 2 chained sessions).
  • Extended history retention (Free: 1 / 7 days → Pro: 30 days / unlimited).
  • No advertisements.
